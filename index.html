About Me

Mendy Cohen is a Financial Operations Analyst at BNY, where he leads AI-driven transformation across Treasury Services. He builds and deploys agentic solutions using Microsoft Copilot and BNY's proprietary LLM, partners with McKinsey on enterprise-wide change strategy, and delivers performance dashboards that drive decisions for senior leadership.

His work — automating complex operations, reengineering legacy processes, driving adoption of emerging technology across large organizations — is directly transferable to defense and aerospace. These industries need people who can bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and operational execution at scale. That's exactly where he wants to be, applying his skills to the missions that protect and advance our national security.

Mendy holds a BA in Economics from the University of Pittsburgh and a background in Business Finance from Touro University.
